López Obrador, whose full name is Andrés Manuel López Obrador, is a Mexican politician who has served as the President of Mexico since December 1, 2018. He is commonly referred to by his initials AMLO. López Obrador is a member of the National Regeneration Movement (MORENA) party and he ran for president on a platform focused on tackling corruption, reducing inequality, and addressing issues related to poverty and violence in Mexico.
